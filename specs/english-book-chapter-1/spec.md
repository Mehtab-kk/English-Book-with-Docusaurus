# Feature Specification: Write Chapter 1 for English Improvement Book

**Feature Branch**: `write-chapter-1`
**Created**: 2025-12-01
**Status**: Draft
**Input**: User description: "Write Chapter 1 for my English improvement book. Title: “Introduction: Why English Matters”. Explain why English is important, how simple English can change daily life, and what the reader will learn in this book. Use simple English and give 2 examples + 3 small exercises."

## User Scenarios & Testing *(mandatory)*

### User Story 1 - Write Chapter 1: "Introduction: Why English Matters" (Priority: P1)

This user story describes the creation of the first chapter for the English improvement book. The chapter will introduce the importance of English, how simple English can positively impact daily life, and provide an overview of the book's content.

**Why this priority**: This is the foundational chapter of the book, essential for setting the stage and engaging the reader.

**Independent Test**: The chapter can be fully tested by verifying that it meets all content, style, length, example, and exercise requirements as specified.

**Acceptance Scenarios**:

1.  **Given** the request for Chapter 1, **When** the chapter is written, **Then** it explains the importance of English.
2.  **Given** the request for Chapter 1, **When** the chapter is written, **Then** it explains how simple English helps in daily life.
3.  **Given** the request for Chapter 1, **When** the chapter is written, **Then** it outlines what the reader will learn in this book.
4.  **Given** the content, **When** checked for style, **Then** it uses simple English, easy vocabulary, short sentences, and step-by-step explanations.
5.  **Given** the content, **When** checked for tone, **Then** it is friendly and helpful.
6.  **Given** the content, **When** checked for examples, **Then** it includes exactly 2 examples.
7.  **Given** the content, **When** checked for exercises, **Then** it includes exactly 3 small exercises.
8.  **Given** the content, **When** checked for length, **Then** it is between 600-800 words.

## Requirements *(mandatory)*

### Functional Requirements

-   **FR-001**: The chapter MUST explain the importance of English.
-   **FR-002**: The chapter MUST explain how simple English can change daily life.
-   **FR-003**: The chapter MUST explain what the reader will learn in this book.
-   **FR-004**: The chapter MUST be written in simple English, with easy vocabulary and short sentences.
-   **FR-005**: The chapter MUST use a friendly and helpful tone.
-   **FR-006**: The chapter MUST include step-by-step explanations where appropriate.
-   **FR-007**: The chapter MUST include exactly two examples.
-   **FR-008**: The chapter MUST include exactly three small exercises.
-   **FR-009**: The chapter MUST be between 600 and 800 words long.

## Success Criteria *(mandatory)*

### Measurable Outcomes

-   **SC-001**: The generated chapter successfully meets all content requirements (importance, daily life, what to learn).
-   **SC-002**: The generated chapter successfully meets all style and tone requirements (simple English, friendly, helpful).
-   **SC-003**: The generated chapter includes the specified number of examples (2) and exercises (3).
-   **SC-004**: The generated chapter's word count is within the 600-800 word range.
